# FlexBook Brand — Team Implementation Checklist

**Last Updated:** April 5, 2026
**Status:** Ready for Implementation

Use this checklist to ensure all teams are aligned on FlexBook branding before launch.

---

## For All Teams

### Brand Basics (Review Together)
- [ ] **Read:** BRAND_LAUNCH_SUMMARY.md (10 min overview)
- [ ] **Understand:** FlexBook name, tagline, and brand promise
- [ ] **Discuss:** Why FlexBook was chosen over alternatives
- [ ] **Confirm:** All team members understand the target audience

### Brand Core (Essential Alignment)
- [ ] **Tone of Voice:** Curious, encouraging, unpretentious
- [ ] **Visual Identity:** Teal (#14A085) + Orange (#FF9F43)
- [ ] **Key Message:** "Your trip. Your rules. Your price."
- [ ] **Core Value:** Flex cheap flights into real multi-stop trips

---

## For Product & Engineering

### Product Experience
- [ ] Review "Flexibility Over Rigidity" pillar — design for changeable plans
- [ ] Ensure one-action-per-screen principle in flows
- [ ] Mobile-first design philosophy confirmed
- [ ] No-login, frictionless experience validated

### Messaging Integration
- [ ] Button copy reflects active, empowering language
- [ ] Error messages are candid and helpful (not corporate)
- [ ] Empty states use playful, encouraging tone
- [ ] Success states celebrate user accomplishments

### Documentation
- [ ] Product PRD references FlexBook brand
- [ ] Engineering docs mention brand pillars (flexibility, simplicity)
- [ ] Onboarding screens align with brand messaging
- [ ] Feature flags/beta messaging uses brand voice

---

## For Design Team

### Visual Systems
- [ ] Primary color palette implemented: Teal, Orange, Purple, Neutrals
- [ ] Typography: Inter (headers & body), JetBrains Mono (prices)
- [ ] Icon style guide created (travel-themed, rounded, warm)
- [ ] Spacing and layout follow "simple, spacious" principle

### Imagery
- [ ] Brand photography guidelines documented
- [ ] Real travelers (not models) in realistic settings
- [ ] Diversity represented (solo female, BIPOC, older travelers, families)
- [ ] No clichés or overly-polished stock photos

### Logo & Assets
- [ ] Primary logo lockup created (with clear space guide)
- [ ] Icon-only version prepared for app
- [ ] Color variations ready (full color, white, single-color)
- [ ] Logo files exported (PNG, SVG, PDF formats)
- [ ] Minimum size specs documented (40px digital, 15mm print)

### Component Library
- [ ] Button styling updated (teal primary, orange secondary)
- [ ] Card layouts maintain warm, approachable feel
- [ ] Loading states use brand colors
- [ ] Status indicators follow color palette

## For Everyone: Pre-Launch Verification

### 48 Hours Before Launch

**Branding Audit:**
- [ ] All website copy uses FlexBook (not "Fast Travel Assistant")
- [ ] Logo appears consistently (correct size, colors)
- [ ] Tone of voice is consistent across all pages
- [ ] Social media profiles are branded
- [ ] Email templates include FlexBook branding

**Messaging Audit:**
- [ ] Tagline "Your trip. Your rules. Your price." visible
- [ ] Key message pillars reflected in content
- [ ] No corporate jargon or overly-salesy language
- [ ] Real imagery used (not clichés)

**Compliance Audit:**
- [ ] Domain is correct (FlexBook domain registered, if applicable)
- [ ] Legal/privacy language doesn't contradict brand (conversational tone ok)
- [ ] Support email address branded appropriately
- [ ] Social profiles verified and linked

### Launch Day

**Go-Live Checklist:**
- [ ] All systems online (website, app, social profiles)
- [ ] Launch announcement posted to social media
- [ ] Email sent to early access list
- [ ] Founder announcement (personal touch)
- [ ] Team available for questions/issues
